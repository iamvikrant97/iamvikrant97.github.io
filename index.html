Background

The short version.

I started in photography and video back in 2012, building a content brand across Instagram and YouTube and shooting for clients across India. Somewhere along the way I got more interested in the side that makes content actually perform: the targeting, the budgets, the numbers.

So I went and studied it properly, an MSc in Digital Marketing in the UK, on top of a marketing diploma back home. Now I'm focused on the paid side, where I get to use both halves of what I know.
